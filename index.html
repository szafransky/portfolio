About me

I am a business / system analyst with 3 years of experience in the financial sector. I started my path at a foreign university, and then continued it in the country. Skills as an analyst also combine with programming, which allows me to easily contact developers. By participating in foreign projects, I learned to talk to clients, collect appropriate requirements and cooperate on the basis of daily meetings. As an analyst, I know that knowledge of both the business and technological approach gives you an advantage in solving problems. Currently, in addition to developing his skills in modern banking, he is programming in JavaScript React as a frontend and Node.js to maintain the backend.
